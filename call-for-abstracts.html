Call for Abstracts

We welcome extended abstracts from students across disciplines working on responsible, aligned AI.

What We're Looking For

Extended abstracts of 1 page maximum (excluding references), from student authors at any level. Selected authors may be invited to contribute to a book chapter or give a short talk at the workshop.

Submission Guidelines

  • PDF format, 1 page max (references on additional pages)
  • IEEE double column format
  • Submit via the embedded form
  • Original, unpublished or published work
