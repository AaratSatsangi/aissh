About the Conference

Where AI meets its deepest responsibilities

This workshop brings together interdisciplinary voices to examine the most urgent question in artificial intelligence today: how do we ensure that increasingly powerful AI systems are safe, ecologically responsible, and fundamentally aligned with human values?

Through keynote lectures, short talks, and poster sessions, participants will engage with cutting-edge research spanning technical AI safety, sustainable computing, human-centred design, ethics, governance, and beyond.

Safety

Robustness, interpretability, value alignment, and safe deployment of AI systems.

Sustainability

Energy-efficient AI, climate applications, sustainable computing, and green AI practices.

Humanity

Explainability, trust, fairness, governance, and centering human values in AI design.
